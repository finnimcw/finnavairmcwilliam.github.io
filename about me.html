My name is Finnavair McWilliam. I was born in Boston, Massachusetts to Virginia and David McWilliam. I grew up just outside of Boston, with two siblings, Erina and Malcolm. I was educated at a Waldorf School through high school. When I was 15 I felt a calling to go traveling, and ended up spending 6 months in Germany on a exchange program. After my high school graduation, I attended Concordia University in Montreal. After a successful completion of one year of university I decided to take some time to travel and do some soul searching. I spent a year in South Africa, volunteering on a farm just outside of Cape Town, working with mentally and physically disabled adults. Upon arriving back in the U.S. I decided it was time to get to work. I began working in the restaurant industry, where I moved through the ranks, from hosting to bartending and then to management. I began to feel restless again after a few years and decided to move to Monterey, Ca. In Monterey I went back to school. I spent three years at Monterey Peninsula College, and then two years at California State University. I expect to graduate in the Spring, 2017. Traveling has always been a part of me, in between my academic and career adventures I visited Italy, Spain, England, Scotland, Hawaii, and Taiwan, as well as a road trip 10,000 miles and 26 states around the U.S. For more traveling photos visit the gallery.
